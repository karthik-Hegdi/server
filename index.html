Idly, also known as "idli," is a popular and traditional South Indian dish that has gained popularity not only in India but around the world. This steamed rice cake is a staple breakfast item in South India, and it has also found its way into the menus of Indian restaurants worldwide. Idly is celebrated for its simplicity, health benefits, and versatility.

Vada, also spelled "vadai" in some regions, is a popular and savory South Indian snack or side dish. This deep-fried fritter is known for its crispy exterior and soft, flavorful interior. Vada can be enjoyed on its own or as an accompaniment to various South Indian meals.

Vada

Dosa is one of the most popular and iconic dishes in South Indian cuisine. It is a versatile and delectable thin, crispy, and savory pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram lentils) batter. Dosa is enjoyed not only in South India but throughout the country and around the world.
